Zhao Song's website

Bio: Currently, He is a postdoc at Princeton University and Institute for Advanced Study. He obtained his Ph.D. from the Computer Science Department at University of Texas at Austin under the supervision of Prof. Eric Price, in 2019. Over Ph.D. time he has been a visiting student in a couple of schools and research institutes, University of California at Berkeley, Simons Institute, University of Washington, Columbia University, Harvard University, IBM Research, Microsoft Research.
